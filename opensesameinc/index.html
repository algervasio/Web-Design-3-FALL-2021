Let's get straight to the point.

We appreciate good design, but we also value being kind to the environment. Here at Open Sesame, we want to build a platform to allow designers to create and display their work while being eco friendly.

It’s simple. You create the design and choose what you want it to be on (tote, shirt, sticker, etc.). From there, you have two options:

1.) Select the amount of product you want, and get it shipped to your home.

2.) Start a campaign on our website for anybody to access and purchase your designs.

Option 1 vs. Option 2: What should I choose?

We recommend Option 1 for people who want to create a one-time product and ship it to their home in the quantity of your choice. There are no quantity limitations. This is great for instances where you want to create a unique gift for a friend or family member.

Option 2 is for folks who want to put their work out for everyone to see, while still making an earning. You’re able to customize your campaign by choosing your design, your product type, product description,the length of your campaign, and you could even include a link to your website or portfolio.
