A graphic designer & an environmentalist walk into a bar...

No, really. This actually all started in a bar.

We (Ben & Simone, founders.) met at a bar in Los Angeles. We were both interested in starting a business. We got to talking, and landed on providing a platform for people to design their own products using sustainable materials. Once we landed on this idea, Ben drunkenly exclaimed “open sesame!” for absolutely no reason.

Our Vision

Howdy, Ben here.

As a young designer, I remember how frustrating it was to get inspired, create, and then have nowhere to put my creation. I would find myself wanting to put my designs on things like tote bags and T-shirts and potentially sell them. Figuring out how to get the product manufactured was anxiety inducing, what was even more frustrating was the idea of creating an online shop to showcase my work. Open Sesame Inc. is a place where designers can do small and large-scale projects for their friends and family, or for the entire world.

Hey! It’s Simone!

I’ve always cared about the environment and I’ve always admired creativity. Ben and I wanted to create a service that would remedy these ideals. At Open Sesame Inc., we encourage individuality and creativity amongst our users, and we applaud them for choosing an outlet for their work that’s good for the environment too.

We believe in opportunity & sustainability.

We're well aware of how hard it can be to put your work out into the world. Putting your work out can lead to having a lot of questions that you don't know the answer to. Open Sesame inc. aims to simplify this process and provide an avenue for both designers and people who just want to make something cool.

Not only do we want to help you create, we also want to allow you to make a quality product that's good for the environment.
