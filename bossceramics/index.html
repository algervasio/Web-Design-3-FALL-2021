meet brenna boss

founder of boss ceramics

Brenna was born and raised in Napa, California. She took her first ceramics class in high school and has been in love with the medium ever since. Grace graduated from San Francisco State University with a degree in Studio Art/Art History, where she concentrated in ceramics. She focuses mostly on utilitarian forms, thrown and hand built, while also adding sculptural elements to her pieces. The simplicity and functionality of her work encourage everyday use.

Her other passion lies in connecting communities through clay. She adores teaching and hopes to encourage students to develop a deep relationship with clay and a passion for ceramics through her multifaceted teaching approach.
